Dispatch FAQs

Direct answers regarding our heavily organized dispatch zones.

Do you charge a massive travel fee to drive to the edge of your zone?

Absolutely not. We clearly advertise our strict service radiuses precisely because we do not penalize customers for living inside them. Whether you inhabit the sheer center of Orlando or the absolute rural edge of Osceola county, you will decisively pay the exact same flat-rate diagnostic fee. We cover the heavy fuel costs entirely on our end.

If I am totally outside the zone, will you make an agonizing exception?

We unfortunately do not make massive physical exceptions to our dispatch boundaries for standard repairs. Driving an extra hour fundamentally wrecks our rapid response times for the rest of our dedicated zone, severely hurting other suffering customers. However, for a massive full-system installation quote, we will occasionally dispatch a senior estimator slightly outside the rigorous borders.

Are your technicians permanently staged in each specific county?

Yes, we aggressively utilize a geographically decentralized dispatch mechanism. We do not foolishly start every single truck from one central warehouse in the extreme north. Our technicians physically sleep in and aggressively patrol their heavily assigned zones, meaning a technician in Kissimmee is theoretically only minutes down the road when you make your emergency call.

How accurate is your estimated arrival window?

Our arrival windows are ruthlessly accurate due to advanced GPS fleet tracking embedded right into our trucks. You do not have to wait around guessing if we will show up between an utterly meaningless four-hour span. Our dispatcher will text you a hyper-accurate link actively tracking the technician’s truck the exact minute they begin aggressively navigating to your property.

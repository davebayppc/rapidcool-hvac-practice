Maintenance FAQs

Direct answers regarding our priority care system.

Does skipping maintenance actually void my warranty?

Yes, absolutely. Every major HVAC manufacturer explicitly states in their thick warranty documentation that parts are only covered if the system is professionally maintained every single year. If a massive compressor failure occurs and you violently demand a free replacement part, the manufacturer will demand to see rigid proof of annual maintenance service records. Protect yourself immediately by staying rigidly enrolled in our club.

What is physically included in the 40-point tune-up?

We execute a ruthlessly thorough tear-down that covers absolutely everything required by the factory. This includes aggressively flushing your primary condensate drain line, chemically cleaning your outdoor condenser fins, checking freon operating pressures, tightening all high-voltage electrical lugs, and heavily inspecting the evaporator coil for bacterial growth. We leave no screw unturned and no wire unchecked.

How much money will a tune-up actually save me?

A severely dirty air conditioner has to work wildly harder to push air, commanding up to 30% more electricity to operate at the exact same cooling output. By radically cleaning the coils and intensely calibrating the motors, we drastically reduce the electrical draw of the entire system. Between the massive drop in utility costs and the avoidance of catastrophic emergency part failures, the tune-up pays for itself completely.

Can I just hose out the outside unit myself?

While gently rinsing the brutal surface debris off your condenser is perfectly fine, you absolutely should not try to violently deep-clean the internal mechanics yourself. High-pressure hoses will aggressively bend the delicate aluminum fins completely flat, violently blocking airflow and absolutely destroying the heat transfer capabilities of the coil. Let our master technicians chemically treat and comb the fins permanently back to factory specs.

General Service FAQs

Clear, direct answers regarding our holistic service offerings.

How fast can you get a technician to my house?

During a true HVAC emergency in the middle of a brutal heatwave, we typically arrive at a central Florida property within 60 to 120 minutes. Because our logistics dispatch system strictly monitors zip codes, we immediately reroute the closest technician the second your call locks in. No waiting around literally all day guessing when the contractor might finally show up.

Do I need to be present for an outdoor repair?

While we can theoretically perform entirely exterior repairs independently, we absolutely demand that an adult homeowner be physically present to review system diagnostics. We refuse to execute heavy mechanical work without giving you a fully transparent, face-to-face breakdown of exactly what broke and securing your absolute authorization. This protects your wallet and guarantees zero misunderstandings.

Do you service commercial rooftop units?

Yes, we aggressively service light-commercial packaged rooftop units, high-volume server room mini-splits, and strip mall systems. Commercial mechanical failures strictly demand an elevated level of technical prowess, and our master HVAC technicians possess the rigorous certifications required to tackle 3-phase high-voltage setups safely. We get your business instantly back on its feet.

What payment methods do you accept at the door?

We leverage highly secure, military-grade mobile payment terminals that our technicians carry directly on their belts or tablets. We accept absolutely all major credit cards, verified checks, and even direct debit for immediate invoice satisfaction. Furthermore, if you are unexpectedly slammed with a massive replacement cost, we can securely pre-approve you for flexible financing completely on the spot.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a new AC unit cost in Florida?

The cost can vary significantly based on your home size, the SEER rating of the unit, and ductwork conditions. Generally, prices range from $4,500 to $10,000+. We offer free estimates and flexible financing to fit your budget.

Why is my AC blowing warm air?

This could be due to a frozen evaporator coil, a refrigerant leak, a faulty compressor, or even a simple thermostat issue. It's best to have a certified technician diagnose it to prevent further damage.

Do you offer 24/7 emergency AC repair?

Yes! We know how brutal the Florida heat can be. Our team is available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week for emergency calls across Central Florida.

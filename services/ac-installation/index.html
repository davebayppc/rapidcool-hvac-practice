Installation FAQs

Everything you need to understand about upgrading your system.

How long does a full installation actually take?

A standard residential system replacement typically takes our crew between 4 to 8 hours from the moment we arrive to the final calibration testing. We deploy highly synchronized, multi-man crews to ensure the tear-out and installation happen simultaneously and efficiently. You will barely notice we are there, and your house will be cooling down by the early afternoon.

Do you offer financing for these large installations?

Absolutely. We completely understand that a new air conditioning system is a massive financial investment for any family. We have partnered with highly reputable lenders to provide incredibly flexible financing options, including zero-percent interest promotions for those who qualify. This allows you to secure premium equipment today while making highly manageable monthly payments.

What does SEER actually mean for me?

SEER stands for Seasonal Energy Efficiency Ratio, and it essentially measures how efficiently your air conditioner uses electricity to produce cold air. The higher the SEER rating you purchase, the lower your monthly utility bills will significantly drop over the lifespan of the equipment. We typically recommend moving up to at least a 15 or 16 SEER unit to combat the brutal Florida summers effectively.

Will a bigger AC unit cool my house faster?

Oversizing an air conditioner is actually one of the worst mistakes a homeowner can make. A unit that is too large will aggressively cool the air too quickly, shutting off before it has the necessary time to drag the heavy humidity out of the structure. This results in a cold, clammy, and incredibly uncomfortable indoor environment that fosters mold growth across your vents.

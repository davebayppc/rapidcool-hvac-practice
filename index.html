If that specific part fails again within the covered timeframe, we will return and resolve the issue entirely free of charge.

My AC is making a loud grinding noise. Should I shut it off?

Yes, you must immediately turn off your system at the thermostat to prevent catastrophic internal damage to the compressor. A loud metallic grinding generally indicates that the compressor motor is failing or a fan blade has broken loose inside the condensing unit. Shutting it down blocks further electrical strain and will save you thousands of dollars in total replacement costs.
